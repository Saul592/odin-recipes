Picadillo

Description

You'll hardly find a heartier meal than a plate of Cuban Picadillo with some rice and maduros. Great for when you need a savory yet versatile dish packed with protein and carbs. You can accompany the dish with rice, on a sandwich or just on it's own! Take the journey of this traditional Cuban dish with me and enjoy however you like.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Red, yellow, and orange bell peppers
  • Tomato paste
  • Ground beef
  • Yellow onion
  • Green Olives
  • Garlic cloves
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Cut up some potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Put potatoes in a pot of water and start boiling.
  3. Heat some oil on a pan.
  4. Once oil is hot, put in the bell peppers, garlic and onions.
  5. Season your ground beef to your liking.
  6. Place the seasoned ground beef in the pan and cook til brown.
  7. Mix tomato paste with equal parts water and dump into pan. Cover with lid and cook for a little longer.
  8. Once the liquid has evaporated to your liking, it is ready to serve.
  9. Serve with your choice of rice, bread, yucca, maduros, or all the above!
