Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description

Chicken Katsu is an incredibly delicious and perfectly crispy Japanese meal. And best of all? It's easy to make! whether you make it to satisfy a craving or to impress your friends, you can't really go wrong. So preheat your air fryer, get yout panko ready and strap in. In this guide, we'll introduce you to the easy to make, yet sock-rocking delicious chicken katsu.

Ingrdients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Panko
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Any sauce or seasoning of choice (we recommend teriyaki)

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 375
  2. Season chicken to your liking
  3. Crack a single egg into a bowl.
  4. Pour some cornstarch on a dish.
  5. Cover another plate in panko bread crumbs.
  6. Take the chicken thighs and cover them with cornstarch.
  7. Dip the cornstach covered chicken in the egg bowl.
  8. Roll the chicken in the panko. Make sure it is thoroughly covered.
  9. Put pieces of chicken covered in panko into the air fryer.
  10. Cook til chicken reaches internal temp of 165F.
  11. Serve chicken with your choice of sauce.
