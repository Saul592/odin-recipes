Gruyere Mac

Description

Don't have any holiday dishes to wow your friends and family? Look no further. Gruyere mac is both easy to make and incredibly tasty. We'll use 3 cheeses including Grueyre itself, DUH! Try and grate the cheese yourself if possible (It really does make a huge difference). we'll cook the pasta til about aldente texture and make a cheese roux which we'll mix together. Once everything is put together, we'll pop it in the oven and put some panko crumbs on that sucker. And with all that, you've made and incredibly delicious dish for the holidays! ... tho not for the Lactose intolerant.

Ingredients

  • Gruyere
  • Cheddar
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Pasta of choice
  • salt
  • Whole Milk
  • Butter
  • Smoke paprika
  • Panko
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Set pasta to boil til aldente
  3. Heat some butter in a pan and periodically stir in flour to make a roux
  4. When the roux is golden brown, pour in whole milk slowly while stirring
  5. Mix in the different cheese slowly while stirring til thick
  6. Pour in all your spices
  7. Take the pasta and pour it into the cheese mix
  8. Pour the gruyere pasta into an oven safe baking pan; cover the top with panko and put it in the oven
  9. Take it out of the oven when the top is golden brown
  10. Enjoy your delicious creation!
